How to Play

Objective

Navigate through dark mazes to collect stars and reach the exit. Use echo pulses to reveal your surroundings.

Controls

  • WASD / Arrow Keys: Move your character
  • Spacebar: Send echo pulse
  • ESC: Pause game

Echo Mechanics

Your vision is limited! Send echo pulses to temporarily reveal maze walls and paths within 15 blocks.

Scoring

  • Complete level: 1 star
  • Collect all items: 2 stars
  • Save echo uses: 3 stars
